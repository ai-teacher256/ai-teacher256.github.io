About DK’s Library

DK’s Library was created to provide free and easy access to books, notes, and teaching materials for pupils (P.1–P.7), secondary students (S.1–S.6), tertiary learners, and teachers. The library was designed with simplicity in mind, so anyone can browse shelves and access resources without needing an account or login.

The main purpose of this library is to support learners in their academic journey, reduce barriers to information, and promote digital literacy in schools and communities.
